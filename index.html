I am a postdoctoral fellow in the School of Computing and Information Systems at University of Melbourne, working with Prof. Bach Le. Prior to that, I was a postdoctoral researcher and pursued my Ph.D. degree in the SnT, University of Luxembourg under the supervision of Prof. Tegawendé F. Bissyandé and Prof. Jacques Klein. During my post in Luxembourg, I visited and collaborated with Prof. Claire Le Goues as a visiting scholar at Carnegie Mellon University, and Prof. Shing-Chi Cheung as a visiting Ph.D. student at Hong Kong University of Science and Technology. I worked as a machine learning researcher at Tencent and Didi.
